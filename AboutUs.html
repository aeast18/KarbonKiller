Baked Goods that Do Good

Our Mission

We use baking to instill a sense of self worth and an awareness of ability to contribute to society in individuals with developmental disabilities.

Our Purpose

Mental health needs in Vermont and in the United States as a whole, are not being sufficiently met. Too often people are labeled and written off, and it is our goal to instead embrace and support individuals with developmental disabilities. We hope to demonstrate that our participants are capable of being contributing members of society despite their differences.

Our Method

We offer a flexible several week baking course. The classes are small and we have multiple intructors to ensure that there is always both a step by step guided demonstration as well as addtional one on one assistance available.
